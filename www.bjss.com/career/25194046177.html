    About Us

    We’re an award-winning innovative tech consultancy - a team of creative problem solvers. Since 1993 we’ve been finding better, more sustainable ways to solve complex technology problems for some of the world’s leading organisations and delivered solutions that millions of people use every day. 

    In the last 30 years we won several awards, including a prestigious Queen’s Award for Enterprise in the Innovation category for our Enterprise Agile delivery approach.  

    Operating from 26 locations across the world, we bring together teams of creative experts with diverse backgrounds and experiences, who enjoy working and learning in our collaborative and open culture and are committed to world-class delivery.  

    We want to continue to grow our team with people just like you! 

    We’re looking for cloud architects with broad technical knowledge, full life-cycle project experience and excellent communication skills.

    About the Role

    As a BJSS Cloud Architect you’ll lead by example and demonstrate the art of the possible.

    You’ll be a technical authority at the centre of our engagements, managing technical risk and creating real business value.

    You’ll be willing to be hands-on when you need to be and seek to balance innovation and intellectual rigour with realism.

    About You

    You have experience in at least one of green-field application development, cloud migrations, data analytics or machine learning using Google Cloud Platform (GCP).

    You are GCP Professional or Associate certified.

    You have the expertise and confidence to work with a range of partners across diverse business and technology communities.

    You have energy and a pragmatic delivery-focused attitude that wins the respect of those around you and makes you a trusted technical advisor.

    You love technology and want to keep on learning. You’re genuinely interested in understanding the challenges your users face and how you can help them.
