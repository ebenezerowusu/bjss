About this Module

This paper examines how architectural approaches have developed to support modern delivery, but also drive approaches in their own rights.

It also addresses the considerations of BJSS delivery, and how architectural-centric has been enshrined as a core principle since the first version of Enterprise Agile.

We cover:

  • The evolutionary nature of software architecture
  • The difference between architects and architecture in guiding the delivery process
  • The benefits of agile architecture
  • Anti-patterns to avoid
