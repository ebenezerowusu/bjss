    FAQS

    What type of clients do you work with in capital markets?
    We work with a broad range of participants across the industry, including investment banks, brokers, trading venues, asset managers and hedge funds. We bring a strong understanding of the entire trading lifecycle across all asset classes.
    What services do you offer to capital markets clients?
    We offer a full range of core and tightly integrated services that combine to deliver transformation outcomes for our clients: Consulting, Design, Engineering, Cloud & Platform, Data & Analytics, and Managed Service.
    How is data science applied in investment banking?
    Data and analytics is transforming the industry and can be applied across the entire value chain to improve business results.
