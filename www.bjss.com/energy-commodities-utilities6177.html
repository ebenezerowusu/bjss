    Why BJSS?

    What services do you offer?

    In additional to our services, BJSS, in response to ongoing customer demand, has developed a range of specific services tailored to the energy industry. See our Oil & Gas - Upstream and Midstream, Energy & Commodities Trading and Energy Retail & Utilities pages for more information. To find out how our comprehensive experience across the industry positions us well to support your organisation's technology goals, please get in touch.

    What experience do you have in the industry?

    We have over 20 years’ experience delivering a wide variety of digital transformation initiatives in the Energy, Commodities and Utilities industry. We have worked with a number of leading organisations from across the industry, including two global supermajors, the world's leading commodities trading organisations, and several of the UK’s leading energy providers. Speak to us now to find out how we can help your business.

    Do you only build bespoke software, or can you support our third-party systems?

    BJSS has worked with many organisations, specifically with teams from their trading portfolios, to ensure that they are seeing maximum value from their ETRM and CTRM systems, whether they are bespoke, in-house built or provided by third-party technology organisations. To find out more about our work in this area and find out how we can support you, please get in touch.

    Can you help with our sustainability efforts?

    BJSS has a dedicated team responsible for sustainability and process improvement. Our team can help you to drive sustainability across your IT estate and help you achieve your net-zero ambitions. Contact us to learn more.

    Ben Cooper – Sales Lead, Energy, Commodities & Utilities
