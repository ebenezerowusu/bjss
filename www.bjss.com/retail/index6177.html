Not only are they navigating cut-throat financial difficulties, but they’re facing significant operational, supply chain, inventory and fulfilment challenges too. Servicing this increasing complexity is hard, so agility and expertise is needed. Our team of experienced retail consultants provide digital and IT consulting services to many of the world’s top retailers. From leading grocers to fashion houses, beauty and logistics providers. They routinely deliver innovative strategies, rapid turnkey processes and next-generation technology solutions.

    Reshape your retail business and its value chain with automation, creating fewer layers and a more efficient workforce that is empowered by real-time data and analytics.

    Automation helps retailers to reinvent and continuously improve their offerings and experiences to meet evolving customer expectations.

    With BJSS’ expertise, retailers can rely on automation to become more than just another technology tool. It will create a key enabler for lasting, transformative change. This can be achieved quickly and effectively, often using legacy systems and existing technology investments.

    We achieve Automation through Technology & Engineering and Data & AI.

    DIGITAL INNOVATION

    Advance your functional performance through better technology and advanced analytics.

    It is crucial to maintain the health and resilience of your technology estate. It has a clear and fundamental role in enabling your operation and the relationships with your customers.

    Put simply: technology enables engagement, education and experience. And in our post-Covid-19 world, these three areas will be of even greater importance. It enables broad gains in productivity, enhances speed and efficiency across a range of services and products and provides significant value through increased revenues and reduced costs.

    BJSS helps retailers to digitally transform into agile organisations. We redefine the end-to-end customer journey to enable retailers to anticipate needs, adapt and evolve. Then we deliver modern IT systems, in addition to unifying data and optimising the supply chain.

    We achieve digital innovation through Technology & Engineering, Cloud & Platform, Managed Service, Data & AI, Strategy and Design.

    Deep Data Capabilities

    Generate accurate data sets to help boost sales and understand customer behaviour, reward their loyalty and increase personalisation.

    Retailers are continuously looking for new ways to reinvent themselves and boost sales. But part of the answer lies in something you already own – data.

    Accurate data will be vital for your business when it comes to understanding customer behaviour, rewarding their loyalty and offering increased personalisation.

    Our talented team of data scientists and data engineers can help you create customer datasets, artificial intelligence and machine learning, so you can target products and offers more effectively. We’ll also help you to maintain appropriate stock levels and improve your customer service.

    Social Commerce

    Converge your retail business with social media so that your consumers can connect with your brand and products more easily.

    Consumers demand convenience more than ever before. And retailers must be able to provide a seamless experience to satisfy these needs. Servicing this increasing complexity and volatility is expensive, and there are constraints in terms of human and physical capacity.

    Social commerce is helping to create a seamless experience by reducing the friction between the start of the shopping journey right through to product selection. Our retail technology consultants will help you adopt this buying channel which is set to go mainstream in the next few years.
