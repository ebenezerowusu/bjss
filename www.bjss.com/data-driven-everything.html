Who is it for?

Data Driven Everything is for any organisation that wants to develop a strong data analytics capability, but has struggled due to the following challenges:

  • Understanding what great looks like
  • Identifying and prioritising uses cases
  • Securing sponsorship and defining the business case
  • Creating a data-driven culture
  • Building the data skills and capabilities in-house
  • Data privacy, security, compliance, and governance
