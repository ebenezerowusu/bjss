BJSS recognised as a Sunday Times HSBC International Track 200 company following its continued global expansion

Written by Chloe Coggins | Feb 21, 2021 11:51:15 AM

BJSS has been recognised in the 12th annual Sunday Times HSBC International Track 200 league table. This follows the company's continued global expansion, having recently opened offices in Houston, Melbourne, and Lisbon.

Founded in Leeds in 1993, BJSS has grown into a company of more than 1,500 employees spread across 15 offices in the UK, US, Australia and Europe.

The Sunday Times HSBC International Track 200 ranks Britain's mid-market private companies with the fastest-growing international sales, measured over their latest two years of available accounts. It is compiled by Fast Track and published in The Sunday Times.

BJSS Managing Director, Stuart Bullock, comments: "International expansion continues to be a key focus of ours. We now employ staff in Lisbon, Melbourne, Houston and New York. Our inclusion in the Sunday Times HSBC International Track 200 is a fantastic achievement. It is the result of the hard work, focus and technical excellence of our people."
