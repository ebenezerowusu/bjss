BJSS continues to supply the UK Government through new G-Cloud 8 framework

Written by David Clark | Aug 9, 2016 4:34:48 PM

BJSS, the award-winning delivery-focused IT Consultancy, has successfully renewed its long-standing G-Cloud supplier agreement with the UK Government.

Part of Government’s Digital Marketplace, G-Cloud is an initiative of the Crown Commercial Service (CCS) which acts on behalf of the Crown to drive savings for the taxpayer and improve the quality of commercial and procurement activity.

The agreement supports the Government’s policy to centrally manage the procurement of common goods and services through an integrated commercial function at the heart of government.

BJSS has supplied the Government and its agencies since G-Cloud was first introduced in 2012. The company has successfully delivered solutions to the NHS, FCO, DVSA, DEFRA, DVLA and Disclosure Scotland, and through the current iteration of the Digital Marketplace, BJSS adds to its existing portfolio of services.

Glynn Robinson, BJSS Managing Director, says: “As one of the leading suppliers to the UK Government via the CCS G-Cloud procurement framework, BJSS is pleased that we will be able to build on our track record of successful delivery and that we will continue to provide our award-winning services to a growing number of public sector clients.”
