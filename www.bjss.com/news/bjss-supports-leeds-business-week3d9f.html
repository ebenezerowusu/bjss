BJSS supports Leeds Business Week

Written by David Clark | Sep 19, 2015 11:01:58 PM

BJSS today announces that it is a key sponsor of Leeds Business Week, the UK’s largest business programme. The award-winning, delivery-focused IT consultancy is also the sole sponsor of the Leeds Business Lunch, which takes place on the 15th October.

Running from 12th – 16th October, Leeds Business Week offers over 100 business-related events providing access to a host of first-class delivery partners, key events and a large line-up of seminars, webinars, workshops, exhibitions, conferences and product launches. Most events are free to attend and the programme usually welcomes around 3,000 contributors, global experts and delegates from all industries.

Frazer Robinson, Head of Marketing for BJSS, says: “We’re delighted to support Leeds Business Week. It’s a great opportunity to build our network and connect with the variety of businesses in the region.”

With over 20 years’ software delivery and IT advisory experience, BJSS is renowned for technical excellence, cost-effective delivery and its proven BJSS Enterprise Agile delivery approach.

Geoff Shepherd, Founder of Business Membership Organisation, The Yorkshire Mafia, says: “We are pleased to welcome an internationally renowned and respected company such as BJSS to help celebrate business in the region. It highlights the importance of informal networking to build successful relationships.”
