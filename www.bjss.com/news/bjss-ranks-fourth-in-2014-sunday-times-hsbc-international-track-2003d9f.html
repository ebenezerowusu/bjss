BJSS Ranks Fourth in 2014 Sunday Times HSBC International Track 200

Written by David Clark | Jul 12, 2014 11:00:35 PM

BJSS, the delivery-focused IT consultancy, has been recognised as Britain's fourth fastest-growing private company for international sales in the Sunday Times HSBC International Track 200.

Established in 1993 in Leeds, BJSS is an award-winning IT consultancy with a growing international presence. The company is recognised for its experience in financial services, commodity trading, media & telecoms, retail, and government sectors.

The Sunday Times HSBC International Track 200 ranks Britain's private companies by fastest-growing overseas sales. The report is produced yearly by Fast Track, an Oxford-based research firm that tracks Britain's most dynamic unquoted companies and has compiled league tables in The Sunday Times for the past seventeen years. BJSS' average growth in international sales was 180.79% during the qualifying reporting period.

Glynn Robinson, BJSS Managing Director, comments: "Our growth outside the UK was initially driven by the needs of existing clients who were keen for us to replicate our delivery approach in their overseas operations. This growth is characterised by a relentless focus on consistent, high-quality delivery and technical excellence and I'm very pleased that Fast Track have recognised this achievement in the International Track 200."
