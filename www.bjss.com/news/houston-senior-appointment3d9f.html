BJSS announces a new senior appointment for its growing Houston operation

Written by Adam Roche | Dec 7, 2020 2:00:00 PM

BJSS, the British-headquartered technology and engineering consultancy announces the appointment of Louisa Keyes Marlin as its Houston Head of Delivery.

Bringing over 15 years’ client engagement and technology delivery experience to BJSS, Louisa most recently worked as a principal consultant at the project management consultancy MI-GSO-PCU3ED where she spearheaded the company’s growth strategy for its Houston-based Fortune 500 account and surrounding markets.

Commenting on the appointment, Amir Soufizadeh, Head of International Expansion, BJSS, says: “As we continue our growth plan in North America, we’re delighted to have Louisa lead our fledgling Houston operation. Louisa’s experience in the Texas market, coupled with her talent in technology delivery and engagement, are hugely significant as we embark on the next phase of our growth and expansion in Texas.”

In addition to her professional responsibilities, Marlin is a certified Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe) Scrum Master (SM) and Release Train Engineer (RTE). She is also a member of the Project Management Institute (PMI), the National Association of Women MBAs, and the Princeton Alumni Association.

BJSS is renowned for delivering high quality, world-class technology solutions to the world’s leading organisations. Houston is the company’s second American operation.
