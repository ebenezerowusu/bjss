BJSS supports Leeds Business Week 2016

Written by David Clark | Aug 22, 2016 11:26:06 AM

BJSS, the award-winning delivery-focused IT consultancy, announces sponsorship of Leeds Business Week, the UKs largest business programme.

Running from 17th to 21st October 2016, Leeds Business Week will offer over 100 business-related events providing access to a host of first-class delivery partners and a large line-up of seminars, webinars, workshops, exhibitions, conferences and product launches.

In addition to sponsoring 'The Big Drinks Evening!', a relaxed, sales-free networking event, BJSS will also host four free-to-attend events at its Leeds Project Delivery Centre.

Mike Buck, Business Head for BJSS in Leeds, says: “We’re thrilled to once again support Leeds Business Week and we look forward to engaging with the 3,000 contributors, global experts and delegates it attracts.”

Geoff Shepherd, Founder of business membership organisation, The Yorkshire Mafia, says: “Leeds Business Week is all about building relationships. We are pleased to have established and continue our relationship with the internationally renowned and respected company BJSS, and we look forward to BJSS joining our celebrations as sponsors at Leeds Business Week 2016.”
