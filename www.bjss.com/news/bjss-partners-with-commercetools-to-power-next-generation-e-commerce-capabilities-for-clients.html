    BJSS Partners with commercetools to Power Next-Generation E-commerce Capabilities For Clients

    14 Apr, 2021

    BJSS today announces its partnership with commercetools, the next-generation commerce software company, to empower B2B and B2C clients to create innovative e-commerce journeys. After a year of dramatic shifts in consumer shopping behaviours, the partnership means BJSS’ retail clients will be able to rapidly adopt industry-leading digital commerce solutions that meet end customers’ expectations. 

    The partnership announcement comes after the reopening of non-essential retail in England on 12th April, a time when retailers are under pressure to provide consistent digital shopping experiences across online and in-store. The Microservice, API-first, Cloud-based, Headless (MACH) architecture commercetools is built on enables retailers to overcome the rigidity of their existing technology stack. Thus they can support new omnichannel journeys more easily.  

    BJSS brings to the partnership extensive experience in the end-to-end digital transformation process, from strategy and service design to digital delivery, engineering and data. With its award-winning Enterprise Agile methodology, BJSS will be able to tailor commercetools’ solutions to support retail clients’ strategic goals and then rapidly and effectively deploy those technologies to the benefit of end-users. 

    David Gore, Head of Industries at BJSS, says: “We’re hugely excited to be partnering with commercetools, a rapidly-growing global leader with a great breadth and depth of knowledge across both MACH architecture and digital commerce. Combined with BJSS’ exceptional track record for designing and delivering user-centric software solutions, this partnership means we can offer even more solutions that help clients transform how they do business.” 

    Dirk Weckerlei, Global VP of Channel and Alliances at commercetools, says: “BJSS is one of the leading providers of technology and engineering consulting in the retail and consumer industries. This partnership allows us to deliver best in class integration services for enterprise commerce customers, expand our footprint into high growth regions, and scale our business both in the UK and internationally.” 

    In celebration of its partnership with commercetools, BJSS has launched a white paper entitled Retail E-commerce: Laying the Foundations for Growth, which is available to download here.
