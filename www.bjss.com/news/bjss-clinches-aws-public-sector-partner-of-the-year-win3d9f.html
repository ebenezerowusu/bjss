News - BJSS clinches AWS Public Sector Partner of the Year win

Written by David Clark | Sep 10, 2019 9:11:25 AM

BJSS, the Queen’s Award for Enterprise-winning IT and Business Consultancy, has today been announced as the winner of the Public Sector Partner of the year at the Amazon Web Services (AWS) Partner Network (APN) Awards.

This win recognises BJSS’ commitment to AWS customers and is testament to the expertise of the Company’s delivery teams and its investment in AWS technology. BJSS has completed many AWS-based engagements across the UK Public Sector over the past several years – including significant deliveries for Disclosure Scotland, the NHS and the Home Office.

The awards, which take place during this week’s annual AWS Partner Summit, honour the partners that are leaders in the network and play key roles in helping customers drive innovation and build solutions on AWS. They recognise a wide range of AWS Consulting and Technology Partners, whose business models have embraced specialisation and collaboration over the past year.
