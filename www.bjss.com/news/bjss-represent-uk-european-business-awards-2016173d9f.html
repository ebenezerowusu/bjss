BJSS to Represent UK in European Business Awards 2016/17

Written by David Clark | Sep 6, 2016 3:48:05 AM

Français
Español

BJSS, the award-winning delivery-focused IT consultancy, has once again been named a National Champion for the United Kingdom in The European Business Awards, sponsored by RSM.

This is the third consecutive year that the company has been selected to represent the UK in Europe’s largest business competition. In the 2015/16 contest, BJSS was also awarded a Ruban d’Honneur.

The Awards, now in their 10th year, are supported by business leaders, academics and political representatives from across Europe, and this year engaged with over 33,000 businesses from 34 countries. All EU member markets were represented plus Turkey, Norway, Switzerland, Serbia and the Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia. Their combined revenue exceeded €1.2 trillion employing over 2.5 million people.

Adrian Tripp, CEO of the European Business Awards said: “Congratulations to BJSS who have been selected to represent their country as National Champions. They are central to the success of Europe’s strong business community, and have shown the core principles we look for of innovation, ethics and success.”

Glynn Robinson, BJSS Managing Director said: “We’re thrilled to have once again been chosen to represent the United Kingdom as a National Champion in the European Business Awards. Our sustained success is an acknowledgement of our business approach, the commitment of our people, and our continued community investments in youth and education.”
