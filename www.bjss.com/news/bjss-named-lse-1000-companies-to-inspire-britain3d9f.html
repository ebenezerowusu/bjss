BJSS named in the 2020 London Stock Exchange 1000 Companies to Inspire Britain report

Written by Adam Roche | Nov 3, 2020 4:51:58 PM

The London Stock Exchange Group (LSEG) has recognised BJSS as one of Britain’s most dynamic and fastest-growing technology and digital businesses.

LSEG’s 2020 London Stock Exchange 1000 Companies to Inspire Britain report celebrates 1,000 of Britain’s fastest-growing and most dynamic companies. BJSS was recognised for its focus on organic growth and making sure that it is a rewarding place to work, too.

Stuart Bullock, Managing Director of BJSS, says: “We’re honoured to have been named as one of Britain’s most inspirational companies. Not only does it validate our Great Place to Work strategy, but it also recognises the technical excellence and commitment of our people to deliver enterprise-class technology solutions and digital transformation. Crucially, the report discusses the approach and measures that we’ve taken to support our clients during the Covid-19 crisis. We’re thrilled to have been recognised in this way.”
