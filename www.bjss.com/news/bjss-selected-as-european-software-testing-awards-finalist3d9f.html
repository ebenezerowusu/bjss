BJSS Selected as European Software Testing Awards Finalist

Written by David Clark | Sep 10, 2015 11:12:03 AM

BJSS, the delivery-focused IT consultancy, has been selected as a finalist in the Best Overall Testing Project – Public Sector category of the 2015 European Software Testing Awards.

The programme celebrates companies and individuals who have accomplished significant achievements in the software testing and quality assurance market. BJSS was shortlisted based on the NHS Spine 2 delivery, which has achieved an 89 per cent reduction in response time, daily productivity savings of 750 hours, and cost savings of £21million in its first year.

Winners will be announced in London on 18th November 2015.
