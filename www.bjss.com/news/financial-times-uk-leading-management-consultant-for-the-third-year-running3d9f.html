BJSS recognised as a Financial Times UK Leading Management Consultant for the third year running

Written by Chloe Coggins | Jan 28, 2022 2:33:48 PM

BJSS, the technology and business consultancy, is pleased to announce that it has been recognised by the Financial Times in its ‘UK Leading Management Consultants’ list for the third year running.

The 2022 report, compiled by the Financial Times and Statista, showcases the UK’s leading consultancies as recommended by their peers and clients. In this report, BJSS was awarded:

- Bronze for consumer goods & retail
- Bronze for energy, utilities, & environment
- Bronze for healthcare & life sciences
- Bronze for IT implementation

“Since setting up our Business Consulting team in 2018, BJSS has become an established player in an increasingly competitive market,” says Hannah Tharp, Head of Consulting at BJSS. “Consistently achieving this recognition over the past three years is an incredible achievement which truly demonstrates the quality of our offer. It has been possible thanks to the enthusiastic support of our clients, and the commitment of my fantastic team of colleagues whose continued drive and expertise have been fundamental to this success.”

About BJSS consulting: Created in 2018, BJSS has grown its consulting practice into a team of over 60 industry-leading consultants. The capability is tightly integrated with BJSS’ world-class engineering and design teams of over 2,000 people spread across the UK, Europe, USA and Australia, and rapidly translates strategy into the delivery of full working products.
