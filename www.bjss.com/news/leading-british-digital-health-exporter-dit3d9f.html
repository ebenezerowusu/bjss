BJSS identified as a leading British Digital Health exporter by the UK Department for International Trade

Written by Chloe Coggins | Feb 25, 2021 6:03:43 PM

BJSS, the leading technology and engineering consultancy for business, today announced that it has been selected as a leading British Digital Health company in the Department for International Trade’s UK Digital Health Playbook – The ‘First 100’ companies.

The Playbook has been compiled by the Department for International Trade as a way of promoting British innovators in Digital Health who are ready to go global. By promoting the ‘First 100 companies’ to overseas buyers, the Department is striving to make digital health accessible, affordable and sustainable to countries around the world.

Following a rigorous selection process, BJSS has been listed in the Playbook as a leading System Efficiency & Transformation company. This category includes organisations with proven experience in delivering improvements in the quality of care through world-class digital technologies and information. Furthermore, BJSS has been selected as a leading Data & Analytics company thanks to its experience in helping the health sector make better use of data.

Lauren Bevan, Head of Health & Social Care at BJSS, says: “We are delighted to be included in the First Top 100 list. BJSS has offices in the EU, USA and Australia already and we have ambitious plans for further international expansion. We are very excited by the opportunities this gives us to support other countries to use technology to help with the health of their citizens and healthcare professionals.”

The Playbook, which was launched by the DIT Chief Scientific Adviser, Dr Mike Short CBE, is available to download here.
