BJSS Joins The Google Cloud Partner Advantage Program

Written by Joshua Devenport | Jun 30, 2021 11:50:33 AM

BJSS today announces it has joined the Google Cloud Partner Advantage Program, meeting Google Cloud’s high standards for partners working with its platform.

As a Google Cloud Partner, BJSS can expand its innovative offering of enterprise-ready, cloud-first solutions, including data analytics, machine learning, cloud migration, IoT, and more. BJSS can also draw upon additional support and resources to build Google Cloud solutions that meet the needs of enterprise-scale customers.

Tim Hancock, Head of Cloud and Managed Services at BJSS, says: “Reaching Partner Level shows that we’re investing in and building our Google Cloud offering for customers. Clients coming to us for Google Cloud solutions can be assured that we have the expertise and ability to deliver to the highest level of quality.”
