BJSS Included on the Crown Commercial Service’s Latest G-Cloud 13 Framework

Written by Jonny Muir | Dec 6, 2022 11:36:36 AM

BJSS today announced it has been approved as a supplier on the G-Cloud 13 framework, the Crown Commercial Service’s (CCS) online catalogue where public sector customers can buy cloud-based computing services. BJSS is a part of Lot 3 for Cloud Support and has also been selected as one of a small number of suppliers for the highly-competitive Lot 4 for large scale transformation initiatives. 

Now in its thirteenth iteration, the G-Cloud framework was established to allow government agencies and bodies to more easily procure cloud services such as hosting, software and cloud support through a centralised hub.

BJSS offers 75 services through the G-Cloud framework, including the following new additions for G-Cloud 13:

  • Digital Twin
  • Content Design & Strategy
  • Customer Experience Strategy & Design
  • Design Capability / DesignOps
  • Sustainability
  • Google Cloud Platform Well-Architected Review
  • Intelligent Products

Kam Bhatoa, Head of Public Sector at BJSS, said: “We’re delighted to be included on the latest G-Cloud 13 framework and to continue to offer a variety of BJSS services to public sector buyers. The fact that we now offer 75 services through G-Cloud reflects the depth and breadth of innovation being sought by public sector organisations. At BJSS, we’ve been collaborating with organisations across the public sector to deliver exceptional outcomes for many years and we look forward to continuing to do so through G-Cloud 13.”
