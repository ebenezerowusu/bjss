News - BJSS named Microsoft UK Country Partner of the Year 2019 Finalist

Written by David Clark | Jun 6, 2019 1:17:13 PM

BJSS, the Queen’s Award for Enterprise-winning IT and Business Consultancy, has been named as a UK finalist in the 2019 Microsoft Partner of the Year Awards.

Joe Macri, Microsoft UK, said: "There was strong competition in this category but BJSS’ exceptional work clearly stood out to the UK judges.  The entire team at BJSS should be so proud of the way in which you have driven innovation, business impact and customer satisfaction. All while investing to foster a culture of continuous learning and one that is focused on diversity and inclusion."
