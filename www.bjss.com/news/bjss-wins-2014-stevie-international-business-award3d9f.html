BJSS Wins 2014 Stevie® International Business Award

Written by David Clark | Aug 17, 2014 11:00:35 PM

BJSS, the delivery-focused IT consultancy, has been named as the winner of a bronze Stevie® Award in the Company of the Year – Computer Services category in its 11th Annual International Business Awards.

This award recognizes the BJSS Enterprise Agile™ delivery approach which was developed based on the company's experience of delivering high performance, high availability software systems.

Winners were selected by more than 250 executives worldwide who participated in the judging process from May through early August. "We congratulate all of the Stevie winners in this year's IBAs," says Michael Gallagher, president and founder of the Stevie Awards. "The quality of entries we receive improves every year. This year's judges were rewarded with the opportunity to review more than 3,500 stories of business achievement and innovation from around the world."

Nigel Wilson, BJSS Head of Service Delivery, comments: "I'm delighted that BJSS Enterprise Agile has been recognized in this way. Refined over 20 years, our delivery approach combines the best Agile principles with strong engineering and management practices to ensure predictable robust delivery. The approach is pragmatic and flexible and is proven to scale from small engagements to global enterprise system deliveries."

The BJSS Enterprise Agile approach is described in the book BJSS Enterprise Agile: Best Practices in Enterprise Software Development.
