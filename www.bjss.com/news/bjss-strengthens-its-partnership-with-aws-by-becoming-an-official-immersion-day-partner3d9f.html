BJSS strengthens its partnership with AWS by becoming an official Immersion Day partner

Written by Joshua Devenport | Apr 12, 2021 3:16:41 PM

BJSS today announces its position as an AWS Partner Network (APN) Immersion Day partner – giving its clients exclusive access to workshop content and tools that have been developed by AWS Solution Architects.

This new partnership with AWS further strengthens BJSS’ position as an AWS Advanced Consulting Partner. It can now provide its clients with custom-built materials that address their key questions to help them make the most out of their AWS investments.

Mo Zamzam, Cloud Migration and Managed Service Lead at BJSS, says: “As an AWS Advanced Consulting Partner, we are always looking to broaden our expertise of AWS products and services. By becoming an Immersion Day partner, we now have access to exclusive AWS content, which we can use for our development purposes.”

“Importantly, we can also repurpose and tailor the materials to deliver Immersion Days for our AWS clients, addressing their specific needs. We are looking forward to getting started.”
