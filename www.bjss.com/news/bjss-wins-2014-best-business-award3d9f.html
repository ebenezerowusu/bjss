BJSS Wins 2014 Best Business Award

Written by David Clark | Jul 15, 2014 11:00:11 PM

BJSS, the delivery-focused IT consultancy, has won the Best Information Provider category in the 2014 Best Business Awards for its work with the Health and Social Care Information Centre (HSCIC).

The award recognises companies that have helped clients successfully harness technology to improve customer service or deliver operational advantage.
