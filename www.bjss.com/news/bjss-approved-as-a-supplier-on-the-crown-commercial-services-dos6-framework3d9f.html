BJSS Approved as a Supplier on the Crown Commercial Service’s DOS6 Framework

Written by Jonny Muir | Dec 19, 2022 11:33:08 AM

BJSS is proud to announce it has been approved as a supplier on the Crown Commercial Service’s (CCS) Digital Outcomes and Specialists 6 (DOS6) framework.

The DOS framework allows public sector organisations to easily procure government-approved suppliers to design, build, and provide clearly defined bespoke digital projects and services using an Agile approach.

BJSS' service listing will be available under Lot 1 - Digital Outcomes - to provide digital specialists to deliver outcome-based services under clearly defined pieces of work.

Kam Bhatoa, Head of Public Sector at BJSS, said: “We’re thrilled to be approved for another Crown Commercial Service framework. Through the DOS6 framework, we’re excited to provide public sectors organisations with the digital skills and services they need to drive digital transformation projects that ultimately benefit the public.”
