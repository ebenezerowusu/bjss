About this Module

In this paper, we look at the benefits of Human-centred design for software products. We explain how these methods enrich Agile delivery and are vital to ensure the success of the products and services being developed and how they reduce risk and save money.

We also explore how you can marry design and Agile together to deliver a well-designed, high-quality digital experience for users.

We cover:

  • Human-centred design concepts, including Service Design and Lean Startup
  • UK GDS standards
  • Design roles and team structures
  • Enterprise Agile best practices
