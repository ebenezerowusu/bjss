    FAQS

    What events do you have taking place and how do I find out about them?
    We host a range of events and attend various partner events throughout the year. If you would like to get involved with our event schedule or connect with us, you can find information on our events through our Twitter, LinkedIn and Instagram pages.
    Who do I contact regarding events at BJSS?

    Please contact our events team at events@bjss.com.

    How do I register for an event?

    You can find our events listed on our events page at bjss.com/events. Our events are free to attend so we don’t require payment details to sign-up, all you need to do is fill in our registration form and you will receive a confirmation email.

    I have registered and I have not received a confirmation email, who can I contact?

    Please contact events@bjss.com for any issues regarding registration.

    I would like to partner with BJSS, who shall I get in touch with?

    Please email events@bjss.com for any enquiries related to partnering with us.

    Will the sessions be recorded and available to watch post-event?

    Our events are recorded, please contact events@bjss.com to enquire about receiving access to this.

    Can I post about the event or send it to my colleagues to attend?

    Yes, if you would like to use a specific banner or hashtag then please do get in contact with events@bjss.com and we will be able to provide you with more information.

    I have a query and the above has not answered this, who can I contact?

    Feel free to contact events@bjss.com at any point with any questions you may have.
