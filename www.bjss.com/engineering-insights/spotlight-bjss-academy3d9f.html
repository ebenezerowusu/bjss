What is the BJSS Academy?

The BJSS Academy supports our Early Careers pathways into BJSS, from apprentices and graduates to returners and career-switchers. The Engineering course comprises four weeks of intense, hands-on learning across a range of disciplines, from Agile to data science and everything in between. Also within the Academy roadmap are Data and Platform courses, along with specialist skills-based training for Azure, GCP, Python, and Data Insights.
