    Cloud & Platform

    Cloud is the Enabler of Business Change

    Cloud is the driving engine of digital transformation. The question is how, not if, cloud fits into your business strategy. With our extensive, end-to-end cloud expertise, we can help your business leverage the full power of the cloud to create organisational efficiencies, streamlines processes, reduce costs, and deliver more innovative services to customers.

    Get Started
    Cloud could unlock $1 trillion of business value by 2030

    Our Services

     

    Cloud Advisory

    Helping you create your cloud strategy, defining the role of cloud in your organisation and building a compelling business case for migration. Through a Cloud Readiness Assessment, we map your as-is estate to identify the steps necessary to get you to the next stage of cloud maturity.

    Cloud Platform

    We deliver the foundations, processes and tools needed to get your cloud services up and running at scale. Through BJSS Enterprise Cloud, our comprehensive cloud landing zone accelerator, we provide a framework that operationalises the business of cloud implementation to deliver value faster.

    Cloud Migration

    We carry out the end-to-end process of migrating your applications and data to the cloud without business interruption. Our bespoke, five-step migration framework aligns with all the main Cloud Service Provider’s cloud adoption frameworks and allows you to see business value from the cloud faster.
