about the white paper:

World events since 2020 have led to an e-commerce explosion. Building an effective e-commerce platform is now necessary to survive in retail. In this White Paper, Ralph Robinson, Head of Retail & Consumer Markets at BJSS, explores how retailers can leverage
cutting-edge digital to deliver outstanding e-commerce experiences, drive operational efficiencies and stand out to customers in a highly competitive space. 

We cover:

 
  • How the e-commerce landscape has transformed in the last few years
  • How to use design thinking to create a unified e-commerce for customers
  • The benefits of automating e-commerce operations
  • How to harness the power of data analytics to provide personalized customer recommendations
