    WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT

    We’re not your typical technology consultancy. You won’t find a strict hierarchy or a constrained career path. Your Squad Lead has a pastoral role and is responsible for ensuring that you have a great employee experience, and most importantly, works with you to determine the course of your career at BJSS.

    We attract the best in the business, meaning that you have the opportunity to work alongside some of the most talented people in the software industry.

    We don’t believe in information silos, so we don’t have traditional departments. Instead, we have communities of practice that ensure quality in our work. We encourage you to join other capabilities that are of interest and enhance your personal growth.

    CONSULTANCY LIFE

    Will I need to travel to clients?
     

    We understand that extensive travel and time away from home can be difficult. We have continued to grow our office locations in regions where our clients are to ensure we offer regionally-based opportunities. However, at times a project may require additional support.

    IS THERE A FLEXIBLE WORKING POLICY?
     

    We want to support you in achieving a good work-life balance while meeting our client obligations and delivering great services. While we always try to accommodate various flexible working arrangements, there may be times when project demands and deadlines need to take priority.

    WHAT TYPE OF PROJECTS WILL I WORK ON?
     

    We are tech, tooling and industry-agnostic. This gives you the opportunity to learn in various industries, working with different clients and getting the chance to learn new languages and tooling. You can continuously develop without having to move companies.

    Can’t find a suitable role?

     

    We are a people-first organization and always on the lookout for good people. If there isn’t a role that suits you, or you would just like an exploratory conversation.
