    Your strategic technology partner

    For almost three decades, we’ve delivered high-quality, world-class technology solutions to the world’s leading companies. Our complex enterprise software aligns engineering, advanced technology, user-focused design and a collaborative approach to delivery. 

    We focus on relationships. Our customers trust us because we’re open and honest. When our clients see the quality of our work, our culture and the resilience of our people, we are the obvious choice. We seek the simple path and are straightforward in everything we do. 

    Successful in even the most demanding and complex environments, we are flexible, adaptable and committed to delivering excellence. We work as an independent partner or operate as part of mixed client, supplier, or partner teams. Our commitment to technical excellence and absolute focus on outcomes mean that we routinely succeed where others have failed.

    Our Brands

    One company, two brands. Both defined by quality, innovation and delivery. BJSS is recognized for engineering excellence and SPARCK, our design agency, is at the center of our business.

    BJSS was created when a team of talented software engineers had a shared vision to deliver better IT solutions. We focus on the technical excellence of our people and a client-obsessed delivery culture. Our clients trust us to provide complex technology solutions.
