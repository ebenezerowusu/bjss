    Energy Retail & Utilities

    Unlocking the Value of Digitalisation Through the Delivery of Innovative, Customer-centric Solutions

    As the global energy market faces increasing uncertainty, the consumer demand for cheaper and more sustainable energy consumption has grown. Drawing on our experience in delivering innovative customer-centric offerings for various big-six energy retailers, we can accelerate your digitalisation initiatives and offer your customers rich, personalised insight into their energy usage. This will help your customers reduce household bills and enact more sustainable energy consumption behaviours, increasing customer satisfaction whilst also accelerating your organisation's move to net-zero.

    How BJSS can help you

    Customer-centricity

    Your challenge

    As global energy prices increase and the pressure to meet net-zero targets grows, energy retailers and utilities companies must find a way to increase the interaction that they have with their customers beyond reconciliation or customer service. 

    How we can help

    BJSS' lean product offering can help you to quickly and easily launch user-centric services that offer your customers more personalised insight into their energy usage. This information can then be used by customers improve their energy consumption habits, whilst reducing their energy expenditure and positively contributing towards a greener planet. The ability to offer customers these insights will give you significant competitive advantage and increase customer retention.

    Decentralisation

    Your challenge

    The UK energy market is heavily reliant on fossil fuels. Diversification into more decentralised Low Carbon Technology (LCT) means of generation is crucial, but they come with more variable patterns of energy supply and demand. New technology solutions are needed to bring together decentralised power sources, give visibility into them, and provide more precise ways to move energy across the grid as demand from businesses and consumers requires.

    How we can help

    BJSS can support your organisation in protecting and developing your decentralised energy offerings, while helping you to achieve your zero-carbon objectives by investing in renewable assets. Our Technology & Engineering, and Cloud & Platform offerings can support your organisation in migrating inefficient databases to the cloud, implementing better alerting on key flows, ensuring faster API performance to improve the speed and accuracy of decision making, as well as continuous monitoring of the stability and performance of your assets.

    Strategy

    Your challenge

    In a highly competitive and volatile energy marketplace, organisations must ensure that technology programmes across their IT estate reflect strategic objectives. 

    How we can help

    BJSS Consulting can help your organisation to define your technology strategy and operating model to ensure long-term success and achieve strategic objectives. Our highly experienced Consulting team can support your technology leadership team with a variety of solutions from Target Operating Model Design and Technology Strategy, to Process Improvement and Change Management.
